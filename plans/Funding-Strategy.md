# Funding Strategy

## Potential Donor Types
- Individual philanthropists passionate about regenerative living
- Impact investors seeking measurable social returns
- Community members providing small-dollar support

## Grant Sources
- Environmental and sustainable agriculture foundations
- Trauma recovery and survivor services grants
- Community development funding programs

## Fundraising Goals
- Secure seed funding for the pilot site
- Build ongoing support for outreach programs
- Maintain a reserve for land acquisition and upkeep
